📡Local ADS-Bdump1090 / readsb▸

Local receiver feed (dump1090-fa, readsb, or tar1090). Merges with FR24/adsb.fi; local positions win on overlap. Leave off if nothing is listening — connection refused will spam the logs.

Many flights to highlight on the local radar when they appear in range — not a dedicated follow. Callsigns (UAL123) or tails (D-AIML, N2136U).

Comma-separated ICAO type codes or designations (e.g. A337, B738, A330-743). Matches every aircraft of that type on radar. Clear either field and Save to remove those watches.
